Great Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a beautiful country in Southern Africa known for its diverse wildlife, rich culture, and monumental landscapes such as Great Zimbabwe ruins in Masvingo.


Below are some facts about Great Zimbabwe:
  • There was no mortar used in this massive construction.
  • It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1986.
  • It was the center of the Kingdom of Mutapa
  • The first European visitors arrived at the ruins in 1868.
